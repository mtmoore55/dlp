What you need to know

Before you sign up
Who can drive with Uber?
Anyone with a valid drivers license, personal car insurance, a clean record, and a car. No special drivers license or previous experience required. Your car must have 4 doors (ex. Prius, Fusion, Accord, etc) and meet your city’s requirements (usually 2005 or newer).
Where can I drive?
You can drive with Uber almost anywhere there are riders. Start accepting drivers when you leave home in the morning. If you’re not getting any ride requests, check your city’s map for our primary coverage area.
Do I need a special insurance policy to drive with Uber?
No. Your personal insurance policy is all you need. Please make sure you carry the appropriate insurance to cover your liability to other parties (liability insurance), damage to your vehicle (optional collision insurance) and injury to yourself (health insurance). If you are unsure about your coverage, check with your insurance provider.
Am I covered under the insurance policy Uber carries?
Yes, you’re always covered when you’re on a trip requested through the Uber app. Uber carries liability insurance of $1 million per incident in case your personal policy is not available for any reason.
Once you've started driving
What does it cost to drive with Uber?
We do not charge monthly fees and it’s free to sign up. A small percentage of your fares (anywhere from 5 to 20%) goes toward getting you more riders, better tools, and the legal advocacy we need to help keep your business running. You keep the rest.
How will I be paid?
All drivers are paid weekly. Checks are deposited automatically via direct deposit, and drivers receive a statement showing their earnings for each trip that week.
How do taxes work?
You pay taxes as an individual—there’s no need to register as a business. File taxes as you normally would, and we’ll send you a 1099 form that you will use to report the income you made driving with Uber.
What if I want to take some time off?
You have the freedom to work or not work when you want. You’re the boss. Just log off from your Uber phone and take time when you need it. No need for approvals. No commitment. No questions asked. And when you get back, we’ll have riders waiting so you can start working right away.
